About Me

I’m G.K. Prarthana Sewmini—a final-year Data Science and AI enthusiast at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University, rooted in Homagama, Sri Lanka. I’m the curious soul who sees data as a puzzle, cracking it with Python, Deep Learning, and a twist of NLP.
